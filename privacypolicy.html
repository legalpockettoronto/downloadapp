1. Overview & Security

Zero-Knowledge for Sensitive Data

LegalPocket uses a Zero-Knowledge approach for your most sensitive identity numbers (SIN, passport number, and UCI). These are stored exclusively in your iCloud Keychain with Apple's end-to-end encryption—not on our servers or any third party.

This means we cannot view, read, or share this sensitive data, even if we wanted to.

Other profile information (name, status, dates) is stored on Google Firebase, a secure cloud service. We do not sell or share your data for advertising purposes.

We comply with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial laws.

iCloud Keychain Requirement

To provide the highest level of security for sensitive identity numbers, LegalPocket uses Apple's iCloud Keychain for storage. This requires:

An Apple ID signed in on your device
iCloud Keychain enabled in your device settings
A device passcode or biometric (Face ID/Touch ID) set up

Note: The passcode/biometric is an Apple requirement for iCloud Keychain to function—it's not a LegalPocket app lock.

3. Who Can Access Your Data

LegalPocket Team

We can access your Firebase data (immigration status, location, uploaded documents) for support purposes. We cannot access your iCloud Keychain data (SIN, passport, UCI).

Google (Firebase)

Google stores and processes your Firebase data on their servers. They are bound by their privacy policy and data processing agreements.

Apple (iCloud Keychain)

Apple stores your sensitive data with end-to-end encryption. Neither Apple nor LegalPocket can read this data.

Law Enforcement

If required by law, we may disclose Firebase data. We cannot disclose iCloud Keychain data because we don't have access to it.

Business Transfers

If LegalPocket is sold or merged, your data may be transferred to the new owner.

We Do NOT:
  • • Sell your data to advertisers or data brokers
  • • Share your data for marketing purposes
  • • Use your data to show you targeted ads

4. Deleting Your Data

You can delete your data at any time:

Delete Firebase Data

Profile → Manage Account → Delete Account. This removes your account and all data from Google Firebase.

Delete iCloud Keychain Data

Your sensitive data (SIN, passport, UCI) is stored locally. Delete it through the app or uninstall the app to remove it from your device.

Request by Email

Email legalpockettoronto@gmail.com and we'll delete your Firebase data within 30 days.

Note: Deleting your account is permanent. We cannot recover your data after deletion.

5. Children's Privacy

6. Security Measures

How Your Data is Protected
Data sent between your device and Firebase is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
Sensitive data (SIN, passport, UCI) is stored in Apple iCloud Keychain with end-to-end encryption
Firebase data is stored on Google's secure cloud infrastructure
Sign in with Apple or email/password authentication via Firebase Auth
If There's a Security Breach

If we discover a breach that could cause you significant harm, we will notify you and the Privacy Commissioner of Canada as required by PIPEDA (Canadian privacy law).

Important: While we use industry-standard security practices, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password.
